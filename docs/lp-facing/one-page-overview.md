# MiroFish Fund Governance Edition

Private beta overview for LP, fund manager, IC, and LPAC reviewers.

## One-Sentence Positioning

MiroFish Fund Governance Edition is a fund-operation rehearsal tool that turns
fund terms, capital calls, portfolio events, governance decisions, evidence
trails, and meeting materials into an auditable simulation workspace.

## Why It Exists

Fund operations are not only spreadsheets. Real governance work often depends on
whether the GP, LPs, IC, LPAC, legal terms, evidence, and reporting sequence are
aligned before money moves or decisions become hard to reverse.

This private beta explores whether a simulation engine can help fund teams and
LP-facing reviewers rehearse that process before using real data.

## What The Current Alpha Demonstrates

- LP subscription, capital call, payment, paid-in capital, and unfunded
  commitment.
- IC approval, investment execution, follow-on reserve, and NAV snapshots.
- Liquidity event, waterfall, distribution, reserve review, and audit checks.
- Governance packet for IC / LPAC review.
- Meeting pack with agenda, decision table, risk appendix, and evidence
  appendix.
- Scenario branches including down round, bridge financing, write-off, delayed
  exit, partial exit, regulatory block, and LP default cure / waiver.
- Evidence bindings that show which terms and outputs came from which source
  references.

## What We Want Reviewers To Judge

- Is the fund lifecycle represented in a way that feels operationally real?
- Are capital call, paid-in capital, unfunded commitment, NAV, and distribution
  outputs clear enough for LP-facing discussion?
- Are IC / LPAC decision points framed in a useful way?
- Does the meeting pack resemble material a fund manager or reviewer would
  actually use?
- Are evidence bindings and audit trails useful, or are they too detailed /
  too thin?
- Which missing scenario would most improve the next version?

## What This Is Not

- Not production fund administration software.
- Not legal, tax, accounting, or investment advice.
- Not a performance forecast.
- Not an investor portal.
- Not a place to upload real confidential LP or fund documents during alpha.
